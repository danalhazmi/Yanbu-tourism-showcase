Yanbu Waterfront

The Yanbu Waterfront is one of the city’s most popular destinations for families and visitors. It offers relaxing walking paths, seating areas, and scenic views of the Red Sea.

The area is ideal for evening strolls and enjoying the coastal atmosphere. It reflects Yanbu’s connection to the sea while providing a modern recreational space.
