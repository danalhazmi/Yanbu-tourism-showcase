Welcome to Yanbu Industrial City

Yanbu Industrial City is one of the most prominent coastal cities in Saudi Arabia, where modern industrial development meets the natural beauty of the Red Sea coastline.

The city is distinguished by its advanced infrastructure and carefully planned urban design, reflecting its significant economic role within the Kingdom. At the same time, Yanbu preserves a strong maritime heritage shaped by trade, fishing, and long-standing local traditions—creating a unique identity that harmoniously blends progress with history.
